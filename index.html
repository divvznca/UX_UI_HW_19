TRIPPLAT

The travel app

Tripplat is a travel app designed for post pandemic travel. The app aims to help its users choose the best destination for their upcoming vacation, based on live reviews provided by other travellers, and also with travel expert support at the destination to make the complete travel experience unforgettable

 

BINGE BUDDIES

The Social Movie Watching App

It's a Strange new normal. Words such as isolation, social distancing and quarantine have become an integral part of the vocabulary with no stigma or taboo attached. In the post pandemic world where it’s difficult for people to meet, Binge Buddy is the alternative to stay connected and have fun all at the same time. If you can’t go to out to the movies, bring the movie theatre home and watch with friends
